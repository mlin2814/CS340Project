Database High School

This site is for administrators to have a central location to keep accurate records of the teachers, students, textbooks, and classrooms within the school.

  • Teachers Page: This page will display all of the teachers in the school, and two forms to add or update teachers. Administrators will be able to search for a teacher in the update form.
  • Students Page: This page will display all of the students in the school, and two forms to add or update students. Administrators will be able to search for a student in the update form.
  • Textbooks Page: This page will display a textbook form that will update or add a textbook for a student. The form has a search bar for the student's ID to add or update the textbook associated with the student.
  • Classrooms Page: This page will display a form that will ask administrators to add a teacher and students to a classroom number
